CRM Data Analyst Case: Maximizing Profit for Marketing Campaign

CRM Data Analyst Case: Maximizing Profit for Marketing Campaign

Overview

The Company: A well-established retail food company selling products from five major categories through three sales channels: physical stores, catalogs, and the company's website.

Objective: Develop predictive models to maximize the profit of the next direct marketing campaign. Implement customer segmentation and analyze data to cherry-pick customers most likely to purchase the offer.

Key Sections

Data Preprocessing and Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):

  • Data Exploration: Initial examination of the data, identifying unique attributes and relationships.
  • Data Cleaning & Transformation: Handling missing values, outliers, and transforming variables for analysis.
  • Data Visualization: Graphical representation of key features, understanding patterns, correlations, and trends.
  • Insights: Detailed insights into demographic patterns, spending behavior, effectiveness of marketing campaigns, etc.

Customer Segmentation:

  • RFM Modeling: Using Recency-Frequency-Monetary modeling for initial segmentation.
  • K-Means Clustering: Implementation of the K-Means algorithm to group customers into meaningful clusters.
  • Cluster Analysis: Detailed examination of each cluster, including density, separation, and silhouette score.
  • Segmentation Summary: Characteristics, response rates, and key insights for each cluster.

Predictive Model Building:

  • Addressing Class Imbalance: Techniques like SMOTE to ensure a balanced dataset.
  • Model Selection: Logistic Regression, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, Extreme Gradient Boosting.
  • Feature Selection: Identification of the most influential features for predictive modeling.
  • Model Training & Evaluation: Training, tuning, and evaluating models using metrics like Accuracy, AUC-ROC Score, Recall.

Key Insights and Recommendations

  • Customer Understanding: Targeting strategies based on marital status, demographics, education level.
  • Previous Marketing Successes: Leveraging responses from past campaigns to refine targeting.
  • Online Engagement: Enhancing online experience to drive sales.
  • Model Performance: Models achieved accuracy rates between 78% and 89%, with varying strengths in recall and precision.
  • Strategic Targeting: Tailored campaigns, online channel leveraging, and precise targeting based on model insights.

Conclusion

The analysis and models provide a robust toolkit for profit maximization in the upcoming marketing campaign. The combination of segmentation, predictive modeling, and feature importance analysis offers actionable insights to enhance marketing efforts and profitability.
