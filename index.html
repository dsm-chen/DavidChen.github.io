About Me

I'm David Chen, a Senior Data Analyst with years of professional experience in data integrity, statistical analysis, machine learning models and methodological review. With a deep passion for analytics, I've dedicated my career to generating insights that drive strategic decision-making.

Beginning with a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics from the University of London, I embarked on a diverse career path that allowed me to explore different facets of the tech world. From leading software development teams to designing a fixed-income brokerage system, automated accounting systems, and streaming platforms, my early years in Taipei laid the foundation for my expertise.

My journey then took me to Hong Kong, where I became a key player in the international trade industry, leveraging data analysis to reveal crucial insights into the trading market. With a focus on Python, Excel, and MySQL, I managed international trade operations, developed expertise in import/export regulations, and coordinated logistics for over a decade.

In my last role, where I applied my skills in CRM data analysis, ETL process implementation, and customized dashboard development. From utilizing regression analysis and clustering techniques to leading successful implementation of ETL processes for banks, my work has significantly impacted retail, telecom, and financial sectors.

Recently, I embarked on a new chapter in Canada, where I look forward to leveraging my rich experience to innovate, analyze, and create solutions that resonate in this exciting new landscape.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me. Feel free to explore my portfolio, and don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions or inquiries.
